NP.location V NP (Location Subject Alternation)
  Syntax: NP[Location] VERB NP[Value]
  Semantics:
    * capacity(E, Location, Value)
NP V NP PP.location (NP-PP; Location-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Value] PREP[in] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * capacity(E, Location, Value)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP.theme (Basic Transitive; Theme Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.destination (NP-PP; Destination-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[in on under] NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
