NP V (Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB
  Semantics:
    * take_care_of(during(E), Agent, ?Patient)
NP V NP (Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * take_care_of(during(E), Agent, Patient)
NP V NP (Unspecified Reflexive ObjectReflexive variant)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient +refl]
  Semantics:
    * take_care_of(during(E), Agent, Patient)
NP V NP PP.theme (NP-PP; Theme-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination] PREP[in] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
