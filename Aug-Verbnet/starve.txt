NP V NP.theme (Transitive; Causative variant)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * suffocate(during(E), Theme)
NP.theme V (Intransitive; Inchoative)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB
  Semantics:
    * suffocate(during(E), Theme)
NP V PP.result (PP; Result-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[to] NP[Result +state]
  Semantics:
    * suffocate(during(E), Theme)
    * Pred(result(E), Theme)
NP V NP PP.result (NP-PP; Result-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[to] NP[Result +state]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * suffocate(during(E), Theme)
    * Pred(result(E), Theme)
