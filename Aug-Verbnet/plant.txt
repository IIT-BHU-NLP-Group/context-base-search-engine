NP V NP PP.instrument (NP-PP; with-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * begin(E, Theme)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP (Transitive; without Destination)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, ?Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, ?Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
