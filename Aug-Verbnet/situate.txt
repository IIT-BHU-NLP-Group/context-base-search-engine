NP V NP PP.destination (NP-PP; locative-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[+loc] NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP ADVP (NP-ADVP-PRED; here/there)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] NP[Destination +adv_loc]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
