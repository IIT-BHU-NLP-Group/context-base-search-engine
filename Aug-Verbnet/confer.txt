NP V (Basic Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB
  Semantics:
    * transfer_info(during(E), Agent, ?Co-Agent, ?Topic)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V PP.co-agent (PP; with-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[with] NP[Co-Agent]
  Semantics:
    * transfer_info(during(E), Agent, Co-Agent, ?Topic)
    * transfer_info(during(E), Co-Agent, Agent, ?Topic)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V PP.co-agent PP.topic (PP-PP; with-PP Topic-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[with] NP[Co-Agent] PREP[about] NP[Topic]
  Semantics:
    * transfer_info(during(E), Agent, Co-Agent, Topic)
    * transfer_info(during(E), Co-Agent, Agent, Topic)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V (Simple Reciprocal Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent +plural] VERB
  Semantics:

    * transfer_info(during(E), Agent_j, Agent_i, ?Topic)
NP V PP.topic PP.co-agent (PP-PP; Topic-PP with-PP)
