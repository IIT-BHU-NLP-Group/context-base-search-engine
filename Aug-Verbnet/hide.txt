NP V (Basic Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB
  Semantics:
    * visible(start(E), Agent)
    * visible(end(E), Agent)
NP V PP.beneficiary (PP; Beneficiary-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[from] NP[Beneficiary]
  Semantics:
    * visible(start(E), Agent)
    * visible(end(E), Agent)
    * Prep(E, Agent, Beneficiary)
NP V PP.location (PP; Location-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[+loc] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * visible(start(E), Agent)
    * visible(end(E), Agent)
    * location(during(E), Agent, Location)
