NP V NP (Locative Preposition Drop)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Initial_Location]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * path(during(E), Theme, Initial_Location, ?Trajectory, ?Destination)
NP V (Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * location(start(E), ?Source)
    * location(end(E), ?Source)
NP V NP (NP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Source]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * location(start(E), Source)
    * location(end(E), Source)
NP V PP.source (PP; from-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[from] NP[Source -sentential]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * location(start(E), Source)
    * location(end(E), Source)
