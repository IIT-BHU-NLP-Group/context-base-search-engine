NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Agent)
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
NP V NP PP.destination (NP-PP; path-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[+spatial] NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Agent)
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(E, Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(E, Agent, Destination)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)

  Semantics:
    * force(during(E), Agent, Patient, Result)
