NP V (Basic Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
NP V PP.location (PP; Location-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[+loc] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Location)
There V NP PP (NP-PP; Expletive-there Subject)
  Syntax: LEX[there] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[+loc] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Location)
PP.location V NP (Locative Inversion)
  Syntax: PREP[+loc] NP[Location] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Location)
NP V ADV (ADVP-PRED; here/there)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Location +adv_loc]
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Location)
NP.material V PP.product (Material/Product Alternation Intransitive; Material Subject)
  Syntax: NP[Material] VERB PREP[into] NP[Product]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * made_of(result(E), Product, Material)
NP.product V PP.material (Material/Product Alternation Intransitive; Product Subject)
  Syntax: NP[Product] VERB PREP[from out_of] NP[Material]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * made_of(result(E), Product, Material)
NP V NP.product PP.material (NP-PP; Causative variant, Material-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Product] PREP[from out_of] NP[Material]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * made_of(result(E), Product, Material)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP.material PP.product (NP-PP; Causative variant, Product-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Material] PREP[into] NP[Product]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * made_of(result(E), Product, Material)
    * cause(Agent, E)
