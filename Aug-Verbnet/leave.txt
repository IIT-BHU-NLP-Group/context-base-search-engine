NP V PP.attribute
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB PREP[in] NP[Result -sentential]
  Semantics:
    * state(result(E), Result, Patient)
    * Pred(result(E), Patient)
NP V NP (Locative Preposition Drop)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Initial_Location]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * path(during(E), Theme, Initial_Location, ?Trajectory, ?Destination)
NP V NP PP.recipient (NP-PP; Recipient-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[to] NP[Recipient]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), Recipient, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
