NP V NP P.asset (NP-PP; Asset-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Product] PREP[for] NP[Asset]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * made_of(result(E), Product, ?Material)
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * cost(E, Asset)

NP V NP (Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Attribute] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * set_member(E, Theme, Attribute)
NP V NP NP (NP-NP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] NP[Result]
  Semantics:
    * designated(result(E), Theme, Result)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * designated(result(E), Theme, ?Result)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), ?Source, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), Agent, Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.source (NP-PP; from-PP)

  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Source, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), Agent, Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Agent)
    * location(start(E), Agent, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Agent, Destination)
NP V NP S (NP-S)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] NP[Result]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * state(result(E), Result, Patient)
