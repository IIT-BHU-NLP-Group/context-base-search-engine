NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Cause] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * limit(during(E), Cause, Patient, ?Goal)
NP V NP S_ING (NP-P-ING-OC; to-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Cause] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[to] NP[Goal +oc_ing]
  Semantics:
    * limit(during(E), Cause, Patient, Goal)
NP V NP PP.goal (NP-PP; to-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Cause] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[to] NP[Goal]
  Semantics:
    * limit(during(E), Cause, Patient, Goal)
NP V NP S_INF (NP-TO-INF-OC)
  Syntax: NP[Cause] VERB NP[Patient] NP[Goal +oc_to_inf]
  Semantics:
    * limit(during(E), Cause, Patient, Goal)
NP V NP (NP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * subjugated(during(E), Patient)
NP V NP PP.instrument (NP-PP; with-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * subjugated(during(E), Patient)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
