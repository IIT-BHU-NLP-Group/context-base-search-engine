NP V PP.destination (NP-PP; to-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[to] NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * confined(result(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP-Dative NP (Dative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * direction(during(E), toward, Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
