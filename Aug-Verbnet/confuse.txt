NP V NP PP.co-patient (NP-PP; to-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Co-Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient, Co-Patient)
NP V NP (Simple Reciprocal Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient +plural]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
NP V PP (Simple Reciprocal Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB PREP[with] NP[Co-Patient]
  Semantics:
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient, Co-Patient)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Stimulus] VERB NP[Experiencer]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Stimulus, E)
    * emotional_state(result(E), Emotion, Experiencer)
NP V ADV-Middle (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Experiencer] VERB ADV
  Semantics:
    * emotional_state(result(E), ?Emotion, Experiencer)
NP V NP-PRO-ARB (PRO-Arb Object Alternation)
  Syntax: NP[Stimulus] VERB
  Semantics:
    * cause(Stimulus, E)
    * emotional_state(result(E), Emotion, ?Experiencer)
NP.cause V NP (NP; Attribute Subject)

    * cause(Stimulus, E)

NP V NP ADJ (NP-ADJP; Resultative)
