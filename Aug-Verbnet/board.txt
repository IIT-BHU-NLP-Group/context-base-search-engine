NP V NP.destination (Transitive; Destination Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.theme (NP-PP; Theme-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination] PREP[with] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V PP.location (PP; Location-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[at in with] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * location(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * location(during(E), Theme, Location)
NP V ADV (ADVP-PRED; here/there)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Location +adv_loc]
  Semantics:
    * location(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * location(during(E), Theme, Location)
