NP V (Intransitive; Plural Subject)
  Syntax: NP[Agent +plural] VERB
  Semantics:
    * social_interaction(during(E), Agent_i, Agent_j)
    * conflict(during(E), Agent_i, Agent_j)
    * about(E, ?Topic)
