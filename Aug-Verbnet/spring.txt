NP V (Basic Intransitive; inchoative)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
NP V PP.location (PP; over-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[+spatial] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(E, Theme, Location)
