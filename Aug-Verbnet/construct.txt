NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Result]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Result)
    * exist(result(E), Result)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.material (NP-PP; Material-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Result] PREP[from out_of] NP[Material]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Result)
    * exist(result(E), Result)
    * made_of(result(E), Result, Material)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.beneficiary (NP-PP; for-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Result] PREP[for] NP[Beneficiary]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Result)
    * exist(result(E), Result)
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * benefit(E, Beneficiary)
NP V NP PP.attribute (NP-PP; as-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Result] LEX[as] NP[Attribute]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Result)
    * exist(result(E), Result)
    * cause(E, Agent)
NP V NP.patient (Basic Transitive; Causative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
NP V NP PP.instrument (NP-PP; Instrument-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
NP V ADV-Middle (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB ADV
  Semantics:
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
