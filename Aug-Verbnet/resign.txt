NP V NP PP.recipient (NP-PP; Recipient-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[to] NP[Recipient]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), Recipient, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP.theme (PP) (Transitive; Theme Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), ?Recipient, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V PP.recipient NP (PP-NP; Recipient-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[to] NP[Recipient] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), Recipient, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V (Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * location(start(E), ?Source)
    * location(end(E), ?Source)
NP V NP (NP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Source]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * location(start(E), Source)
    * location(end(E), Source)
NP V PP.source (PP; from-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[from] NP[Source -sentential]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * location(start(E), Source)
    * location(end(E), Source)
