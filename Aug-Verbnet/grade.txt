NP V NP (NP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * group(during(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.goal (NP-PP; as-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[as] NP[Goal -sentential]
  Semantics:
    * group(during(E), Theme, Goal)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.location (NP-PP; in-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[in] NP[Goal]
  Semantics:
    * group(during(E), Theme, Goal)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP.patient (Basic Transitive; Causative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
NP V NP PP.instrument (NP-PP; Instrument-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
NP V ADV-Middle (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB ADV
  Semantics:
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
