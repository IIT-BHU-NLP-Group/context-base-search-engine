NP.attribute V (Intransitive; Attribute Subject)
  Syntax: NP[Patient] LEX['s] NP[Attribute] VERB
  Semantics:
    * change_value(during(E), Direction, Attribute, Patient)
NP V NP PP.destination (NP-PP; locative-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[+dest_conf] NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
