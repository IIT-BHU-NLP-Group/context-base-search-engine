NP V NP (Basic Transitive; Causative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * contact(during(E), ?Instrument, Patient)
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient)
    * physical_form(result(E), Form, Patient)
