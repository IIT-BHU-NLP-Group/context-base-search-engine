NP V (Simple Reciprocal Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Patient +plural] VERB
  Semantics:
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient_i)
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient_j)
    * mingled(result(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
NP NP V together (Together Reciprocal Alternation Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Patient +plural] VERB LEX[together]
  Semantics:
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient_i)
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient_j)
    * mingled(result(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Product]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP.beneficiary NP (NP-NP; Beneficiary Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Beneficiary] NP[Product]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * benefit(E, Beneficiary)
NP V NP PP.beneficiary (NP-PP; for-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Product] PREP[for] NP[Beneficiary]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * benefit(E, Beneficiary)
