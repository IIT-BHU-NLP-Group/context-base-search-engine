NP V NP PP.recipient (NP-PP; Recipient-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[to] NP[Recipient]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), Recipient, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP.theme (PP) (Transitive; Theme Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), ?Recipient, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)

NP V PP.co-theme (PP; to-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[to] NP[Co-Theme]
  Semantics:
