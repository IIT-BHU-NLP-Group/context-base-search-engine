NP V (Basic Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
NP V PP.location (PP; Location-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[+loc] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Location)
There V NP PP (NP-PP; Expletive-there Subject)
  Syntax: LEX[there] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[+loc] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Location)
PP.location V NP (Locative Inversion)
  Syntax: PREP[+loc] NP[Location] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Location)
NP V ADV (ADVP-PRED; here/there)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Location +adv_loc]
  Semantics:
    * appear(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Location)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Location)
NP V NP ADJ
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB NP[Result]
  Semantics:
    * state(result(E), Result, Patient)
    * Pred(result(E), Patient)
NP V (Basic Intransitive)

    * path(during(E), Theme, ?Initial_Location, ?Trajectory, ?Destination)
