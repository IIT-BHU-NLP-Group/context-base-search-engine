NP V NP to be NP (Infinitival Copular Clause)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] NP[Predicate]
  Semantics:
    * declare(during(E), Agent, Theme, Predicate)
NP V that S (S)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme +that_comp]
  Semantics:
    * declare(during(E), Agent, Theme, ?Predicate)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme -sentential]
  Semantics:
    * declare(during(E), Agent, Theme, ?Predicate)
NP V NP PP.goal (NP-PP; Goal-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[to] NP[Goal]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * future_possession(end(E), Goal, Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP.theme (PP) (Transitive; Theme Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * future_possession(end(E), ?Goal, Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP-Dative NP (Dative)

  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
    * future_possession(end(E), Goal, Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.goal (NP-PP; for-PP)

    * has_possession(start(E), Agent, Theme)
