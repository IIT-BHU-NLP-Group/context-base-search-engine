NP V NP (Basic Transitive; dropped Instrument)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, ?Instrument)
    * manner(end(E), forceful, ?Instrument)
    * contact(end(E), ?Instrument, Patient)
NP V NP PP.patient (NP-PP; through-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Instrument] PREP[through into] NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Instrument)
    * manner(end(E), forceful, Instrument)
    * contact(end(E), Instrument, Patient)
    * in(end(E), Instrument, Patient)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
NP V NP PP.instrument (NP-PP; with-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Instrument)
    * manner(end(E), forceful, Instrument)
    * contact(end(E), Instrument, Patient)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
NP V PP (Conative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB LEX[at] NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Agent)
NP V PP PP (Conative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB LEX[at] NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Instrument)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
NP.instrument V NP (Instrument Subject Alternation)
  Syntax: NP[Instrument] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Instrument)
    * manner(end(E), forceful, Instrument)
    * contact(end(E), Instrument, Patient)
NP V PP.destination NP (PP-NP; upon/on-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[on upon] NP[Destination] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP (Simple Reciprocal Alternation Transitive; prepositional variant)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[with into to] NP[Co-Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient, Co-Patient)
NP V NP together (Together Reciprocal Alternation Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient +plural] LEX[together]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient, Co-Patient)
NP V PP.destination (PP; locative-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[+loc +dir +dest_conf] NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Destination)
NP V NP PP.destination-Conative (Conative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] LEX[at] NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
