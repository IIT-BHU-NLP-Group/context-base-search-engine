NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Product]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * made_of(result(E), Product, ?Material)
    * cause(Agent, E)
