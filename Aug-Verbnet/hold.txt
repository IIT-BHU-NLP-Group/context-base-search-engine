NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * location(start(E), Theme, ?Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, ?Destination)
    * confined(result(E), Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP to be NP (Infinitival Copular Clause)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] LEX[to] LEX[be] NP[Predicate]
  Semantics:
    * believe(during(E), Agent, Theme, Predicate)
NP V that S (S)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme +that_comp]
  Semantics:
    * believe(during(E), Agent, Theme, ?Predicate)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme -sentential]
  Semantics:
    * believe(during(E), Agent, Theme, ?Predicate)
NP.location V NP (Location Subject Alternation)
  Syntax: NP[Location] VERB NP[Value]
  Semantics:
    * capacity(E, Location, Value)
NP V NP PP.location (NP-PP; Location-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Value] PREP[in] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * capacity(E, Location, Value)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP (Transitive; Body-part Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * hold(during(E), Agent, Theme)
    * contact(during(E), Agent, Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
