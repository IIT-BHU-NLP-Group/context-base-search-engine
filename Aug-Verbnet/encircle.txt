NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Co-Theme]
  Semantics:
    * contact(during(E), Theme, Co-Theme)
    * exist(during(E), Theme)
    * exist(during(E), Co-Theme)
NP V NP PP.theme (NP-PP; with-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination] PREP[with] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP (Locatum Subject Alternation)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * location(E, Theme, Destination)
NP V NP.destination (Transitive; Destination Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), ?Theme)
    * location(start(E), ?Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), ?Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
