NP V NP to be NP (Infinitival Copular Clause)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] NP[Predicate]
  Semantics:
    * declare(during(E), Agent, Theme, Predicate)

  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme +that_comp]
  Semantics:
    * declare(during(E), Agent, Theme, ?Predicate)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)

  Semantics:
    * declare(during(E), Agent, Theme, ?Predicate)
NP V what S (WHAT-S)

  Semantics:
    * declare(during(E), Agent, Theme, ?Result)

NP V NP ADJ (NP-PP; as-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[as] NP[Result -sentential]

    * declare(during(E), Agent, Theme, ?Result)
NP V NP (NP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
