NP V NP PP.theme (NP-PP; Theme-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination] PREP[in] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Experiencer] VERB NP[Stimulus]
  Semantics:
    * perceive(during(E), Experiencer, Stimulus)
    * in_reaction_to(E, Stimulus)
It V (Intransitive; Expletive Subject)
  Syntax: LEX[it] LEX[[+be]] VERB
  Semantics:
    * weather(during(E), Weather_type, ?Theme)
It V NP.theme (NP; Expletive Subject, Theme Object)

  Semantics:
    * weather(during(E), Weather_type, Theme)
It V PP.theme (PP; Expletive Subject, Theme-PP)
