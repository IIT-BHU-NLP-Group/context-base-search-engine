NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Agent)
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
NP V NP PP.location (NP-PP; path-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[+spatial] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Agent)
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(E, Theme, Location)
    * Prep(E, Agent, Location)
NP V PP.theme (PP; after-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB PREP[after] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Agent)
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
NP V S_ING (ING-AC)
  Syntax: NP[Experiencer] VERB NP[Stimulus +ac_ing]
  Semantics:
    * understand(E, Experiencer, Stimulus, ?Attribute)
NP V how S_INF (HOW-TO-INF)
  Syntax: NP[Experiencer] VERB NP[Stimulus +wh_inf]
  Semantics:
    * understand(E, Experiencer, Stimulus, ?Attribute)
NP V NP to be NP (NP-TOBE)
  Syntax: NP[Experiencer] VERB NP[Stimulus +to_be]
  Semantics:
    * understand(E, Experiencer, Stimulus, ?Attribute)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Co-Theme]
  Semantics:
    * contact(during(E), Theme, Co-Theme)
    * exist(during(E), Theme)
    * exist(during(E), Co-Theme)
NP v (Basic Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB
  Semantics:
    * occur(during(E), Theme)
There V NP (NP; Expletive-there Subject)
  Syntax: LEX[there] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * occur(during(E), Theme)
NP V PP.location (PP; Location-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[+loc] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * occur(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(during(E), Theme, Location)
PP.location V NP (Locative Inversion)
  Syntax: PREP[+loc] NP[Location] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * occur(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(during(E), Theme, Location)
It V that S (SEEM-S)
  Syntax: LEX[it] VERB NP[Theme +that_comp]
  Semantics:
    * occur(during(E), Theme)
