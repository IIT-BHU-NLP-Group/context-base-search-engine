NP V NP PP.theme (NP-PP; with-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination] PREP[with] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * location(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP (Locatum Subject Alternation)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * location(E, Theme, Destination)
NP V NP.destination (Transitive; Destination Object)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), ?Theme)
    * location(start(E), ?Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E), ?Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V PP (Conative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB LEX[at] NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Agent)
    * contact(during(E), Agent, Patient)
NP V PP PP (Conative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB LEX[at] NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Instrument)
    * contact(during(E), Instrument, Patient)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * attached(end(E), Patient, Instrument)
NP V NP PP.co-patient (NP-PP; locative-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[to on onto] NP[Co-Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient, Co-Patient)
    * attached(end(E), Patient, Instrument)
    * attached(end(E), Co-Patient, Instrument)
NP V NP together (Together Reciprocal Alternation Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient +plural] LEX[together]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
    * attached(end(E), Patient_i, Instrument)
    * attached(end(E), Patient_j, Instrument)
NP V ADV-Middle PP (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB ADV PREP[to on onto] NP[Co-Patient]
  Semantics:
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient, Co-Patient)
    * attached(end(E), Patient, Instrument)
    * attached(end(E), Co-Patient, Instrument)
NP V ADV-Middle (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Patient +plural] VERB ADV
  Semantics:
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
    * attached(end(E), Patient_i, Instrument)
    * attached(end(E), Patient_j, Instrument)
NP V ADV-Middle together (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Patient +plural] VERB ADV LEX[together]
  Semantics:
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
    * attached(end(E), Patient_i, Instrument)
    * attached(end(E), Patient_j, Instrument)
NP V together ADV-Middle (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Patient +plural] VERB LEX[together] ADV
  Semantics:
    * together(end(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
    * attached(end(E), Patient_i, Instrument)
    * attached(end(E), Patient_j, Instrument)
NP V NP ADJP (NP-ADJP; Resultative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] NP[Result]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * attached(end(E), Patient, Instrument)
    * Pred(result(E), Patient)
