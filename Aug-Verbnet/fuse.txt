NP V (Simple Reciprocal Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Patient +plural] VERB
  Semantics:
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient_i)
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient_j)
    * mingled(result(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
NP NP V together (Together Reciprocal Alternation Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Patient +plural] VERB LEX[together]
  Semantics:
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient_i)
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient_j)
    * mingled(result(E), physical, Patient_i, Patient_j)
NP V NP.patient (Basic Transitive; Causative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
NP V NP PP.instrument (NP-PP; Instrument-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
NP.patient V (Intransitive; Inchoative)
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB
  Semantics:
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
NP V ADV-Middle (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB ADV
  Semantics:
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
NP.instrument V NP (Instrument Subject Alternation)
  Syntax: NP[Instrument] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * use(during(E), ?Agent, Instrument)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Patient)
NP V PP ADV-Middle (Middle Construction)
  Syntax: NP[Patient] VERB PREP[with into to] NP[Co-Patient] ADV
  Semantics:
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient)
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Co-Patient)
    * mingled(result(E), physical, Patient, Co-Patient)
NP V NP (Transitive; affected Patient, no Co-Patient)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * degradation_material_integrity(result(E), Patient)
