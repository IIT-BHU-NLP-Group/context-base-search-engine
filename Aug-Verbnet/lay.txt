NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Result]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Result)
    * exist(result(E), Result)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.material (NP-PP; Material-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Result] PREP[from out_of] NP[Material]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Result)
    * exist(result(E), Result)
    * made_of(result(E), Result, Material)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.beneficiary (NP-PP; for-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Result] PREP[for] NP[Beneficiary]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Result)
    * exist(result(E), Result)
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * benefit(E, Beneficiary)
NP V NP PP.attribute (NP-PP; as-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Result] LEX[as] NP[Attribute]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Result)
    * exist(result(E), Result)
    * cause(E, Agent)
NP V NP PP.destination (NP-PP; locative-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] PREP[+loc] NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * position(end(E), Theme, Pos)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP ADVP (NP-ADVP-PRED; here/there)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme] NP[Destination +adv_loc]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * Prep(start(E), Theme, Destination)
    * Prep(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * position(end(E), Theme, Pos)
    * cause(Agent, E)
