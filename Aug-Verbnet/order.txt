NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * has_possession(start(E), ?Source, Theme)
    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), Agent, Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.source (NP-PP; from-PP)

    * transfer(during(E), Theme)
    * has_possession(end(E), Agent, Theme)
    * cause(Agent, E)
