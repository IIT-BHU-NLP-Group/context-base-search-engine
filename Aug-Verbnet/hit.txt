NP V PP.location (PP; against-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB PREP[against into onto] NP[Location]
  Semantics:
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Theme)
    * contact(during(E), Theme, Location)
    * manner(end(E), forceful, Theme)
    * contact(end(E), Theme, Location)
NP V (Understood Reciprocal Object)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] LEX[and] NP[Co-Theme] VERB
  Semantics:
    * contact(during(E), Theme, Co-Theme)
    * exist(during(E), Theme)
    * exist(during(E), Co-Theme)
NP V PP (Conative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB LEX[at] NP[Patient]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Agent)
    * contact(during(E), Agent, Patient)
NP V PP PP (Conative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB LEX[at] NP[Patient] PREP[with] NP[Instrument]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * manner(during(E), directedmotion, Instrument)
    * contact(during(E), Instrument, Patient)
    * use(during(E), Agent, Instrument)
NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Agent)
    * location(start(E), Agent, Destination)
    * location(end(E), Agent, Destination)
NP V NP-Dative NP (Dative)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * exert_force(during(E0), Agent, Theme)
    * contact(end(E0), Agent, Theme)
    * motion(during(E1), Theme)
    * contact(during(E1), Agent, Theme)
    * location(start(E1), Theme, Destination)
    * location(end(E1), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E1)
    * meets(E0, E1)
