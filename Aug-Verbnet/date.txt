NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination]
  Semantics:
    * created_image(result(E), ?Theme)
    * location(end(E), ?Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP PP.theme (NP-PP; with-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Destination] PREP[with] NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * created_image(result(E), Theme)
    * location(end(E), Theme, Destination)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V (Intransitive; Plural Subject)
  Syntax: NP[Agent +plural] VERB
  Semantics:
    * social_interaction(during(E), Agent_i, Agent_j)
NP V NP (Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Co-Agent]
  Semantics:
    * social_interaction(during(E), Agent, Co-Agent)
