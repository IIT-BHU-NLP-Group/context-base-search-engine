NP V how S (HOW-S)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Topic +wh_extract]
  Semantics:
    * transfer_info(during(E), Agent, ?Co-Agent, ?Topic)
    * cause(Agent, E)

  Semantics:
    * transfer_info(during(E), Agent, ?Co-Agent, ?Topic)
    * cause(Agent, E)

  Semantics:
    * social_interaction(during(E), Agent_i, Agent_j)
    * about(E, Theme)
