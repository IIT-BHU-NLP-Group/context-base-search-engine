NP V PP.theme (PP; on-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent +plural] VERB PREP[on] NP[Theme -sentential]
  Semantics:
    * cooperate(during(E), Agent_i, Agent_j, Theme)
NP V PP.co-agent PP.theme (PP-PP; with-PP on-PP)

  Semantics:
    * cooperate(during(E), Agent, Co-Agent, Theme)

NP.material V PP.product (PP; Inchoative variant, Product-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Material] VERB PREP[into] NP[Product]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * made_of(result(E), Product, Material)
    * state(start(E), Endstate, Material)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Material)
NP V NP.material PP.product (NP-PP; Causative variant, Product-PP)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Material] PREP[into] NP[Product]
  Semantics:
    * exist(start(E), Product)
    * exist(result(E), Product)
    * made_of(result(E), Product, Material)
    * state(start(E), Endstate, Material)
    * state(result(E), Endstate, Material)
    * cause(Agent, E)
NP V NP.material (Transitive; Causative variant, Material Object)
