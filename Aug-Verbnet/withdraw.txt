NP V (Basic Intransitive)
  Syntax: NP[Theme] VERB
  Semantics:
    * motion(during(E), Theme)
    * path(during(E), Theme, ?Initial_Location, ?Trajectory, ?Destination)

NP V NP (Basic Transitive)
  Syntax: NP[Agent] VERB NP[Theme]
  Semantics:
    * cause(Agent, E)
    * location(start(E), Theme, ?Source)
    * location(end(E), Theme, ?Source)
